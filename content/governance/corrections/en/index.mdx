# Corrections Policy

## Reporting Errors

We are committed to accuracy and take all reports of errors seriously. If you identify an error in our content, please report it using the "Report Issue" feature available on every content page.

## What to Report

Please report:
- Factual errors
- Outdated information
- Missing or incorrect citations
- Safety concerns
- Typos or formatting issues
- Broken links

## Correction Process

1. **Report Received**: Your report is logged and reviewed
2. **Verification**: We verify the reported error against current evidence
3. **Correction**: If verified, the content is corrected
4. **Review**: Corrected content is reviewed by a qualified clinician
5. **Update**: The correction is published, and the changelog is updated

## Response Time

- **Critical errors** (safety concerns, serious factual errors): Within 48 hours
- **Standard errors**: Within 1 week
- **Minor issues** (typos, formatting): Within 2 weeks

## Transparency

- Significant corrections are documented in the changelog
- Users can see what was corrected and when
- We maintain transparency about our correction process

## Thank You

We appreciate your help in maintaining the accuracy of our content. Your reports help us improve and ensure that Psychpedia remains a trusted source of mental health information.
